Josep Borrell oo weerar ku qaaday Israel, dalab culusna u diray Midowga Yurub

Brussels (Caasimada Online) – Madaxa Siyaasadda Arrimaha Dibadda ee Midowga Yurub, Josep Borrell ayaa sheegay in Midowga Yurub uu wajahayo doorasho adag, oo ku aaddan in ay kala doortaan taageerada hay’adaha caalamiga ah amaba taageeradooda Israel. Madaxa Siyaasadda Arrimaha Dibadda ee Midowga Yurub ayaa sheegay in dalalka xubnaha ka ah ururka looga baahan yahay in ay qeexaan mowqifkooda kaga aaddan amarka ka soo baxay Maxkamadda Cadddaaladda Caalamiga ah ee Israel lagu faray in ay joojiso weerarka ballaaran ee deegaanka Rafah ee koonfurta Marinka Gaza. “Waa inaan kala dooranaa taageeradayada hay’adaha caalamiga ah iyo sharciga ama taageerada aan siino Israel. Waxay noqon doonaan kuwo aad u adag in la is waafajiyo,” ayuu yiri Josep Borrell. Isagoo ka hadlayay kulan uu kaga qaybgalay Magaalada Florence ee dalkaasi Talyaaniga ayaa wuxuu yiri “Aan aragno tallaabada uu Midowga Yurub ka qaadi doono go’aanka ka soo baxay Maxkamadda ICJ, kaas oo noqon doono mowqifkayaga.” Waxa uu sheegay in dalalka Midowga Yurub ay horaan ku kala qaybsameen, marka ay noqoto jawaabcelintooda duulaanka Israel ee Marinka Gaza. “Waxaa jiray khilaaf aad u qoto dheer oo ka dhashay go’aan qaadashada dalalka Midowga Yurub ee dagaalka Gaza. Hadda waxaan joognaa inaan kala doorano taageerada hay’adaha caalamiga ah amaba taageeradayada Israel,” ayuu yiri Josep Borrell.
